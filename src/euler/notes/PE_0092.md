# [PE_0092](../PE_0092.java)

For a given number of digits $d$ (e.g. $7$ digits, when the limit is $10^7$), the highest any number can be after the first step is $d \cdot 9^2$.  

Iterating $n$ from $1$ to $d \cdot 9^2$, we skip if $n$'s chain ends in $1$, and otherwise recursively check for how many numbers their square digit sum is equal to $n$.
