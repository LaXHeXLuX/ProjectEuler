# [PE_0102](../PE_0102.java)

To determine whether a triangle $\triangle(A, B, C)$ contains the origin, we can look at the cross products $A \times B$, $B \times C$, $C \times A$. If the origin is contained in the triangle, then all products would have the same sign (i.e. all positive or all negative). But if the origin is outside the triangle, then one cross product will have a different sign.
