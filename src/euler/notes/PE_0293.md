# [PE_0293](../PE_0293.java)

The smallest admissible number is $2$, meaning $N \ge 2, M \ge 2 \Rightarrow N+M \ge 4$. Then for $N+M$ to be prime, it must be odd. $N$ is always even, so $M$ must be odd.  
All admissible numbers under a given limit $L$ are generated recursively, and their pseudo-Fortunate numbers are found by iterating $M$.

Some results in the form  
`<L> <time in ms>: <distinct M sum>`
